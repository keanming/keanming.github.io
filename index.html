I am currently a postdoctoral research associate supervised by Professors Han Liu and Tong Zhang. Prior to this, I did my PhD studies in the Department of Biostatistics at University of Washington, advised by Daniela Witten. I obtained my BS/MS through a 5-year program from Purdue University prior to coming to University of Washington. I am interested in working on statistical methods for high-dimensional data. In particular, my work focuses on graphical models and clustering for high dimensional data.
