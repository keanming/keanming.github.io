Graphical models with Hubs

[CRAN hglasso package].

An R software package for estimating graphs with hub nodes.

Reference: KM Tan, P London, K Mohan, S-I Lee, M Fazel and Ditten. Learning graphical models with hubs. Journal of Machine Learning Research 15(Oct):3297-3331. [pdf]

Sparse biclustering

[CRAN sparseBC package].

An R software package for performing sparse biclustering of transposable data. Sparse biclustering and matrix variate normal biclustering are implemented.

Reference: KM Tan and D Witten. Sparse biclustering of transposable data. Journal of Computational and Graphical Statistics (23)4:985-1008. [pdf]
